About Mayur

Where Inner Clarity
Meets Professional Excellence

Mayur Dadhich is an accomplished Corporate Trainer, Art of Living Faculty, and Financial Market Strategist with over 12 years of deep experience spanning India's premier financial institutions and Vietnam's vibrant corporate landscape.

"True leadership begins with inner clarity and emotional balance — enabling individuals and organizations to thrive with purpose, innovation, and compassion."

As Founder of Investment Mentor, and with strong ties to India's National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE), Mayur brings rigorous financial knowledge alongside a profound commitment to human development.

He has delivered corporate wellness and leadership programs across Vietnam for organizations including PetroVietnam, Roche Diagnostics, Gem Global, Smart Train Academy, Quang Phat Seal, Medionline Group, and the Consulate General of India — touching the lives of professionals across every sector.

Art of Living

Ancient Wisdom for
the Modern Professional

As an active Faculty Member of The Art of Living Foundation — one of the world's largest volunteer-based humanitarian organizations, founded by Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Ji — Mayur brings time-tested tools of transformation to corporate environments.

These programs go beyond ordinary wellness — they integrate the science of breath (Sudarshan Kriya), yoga, and meditation with modern psychology to produce measurable improvements in clarity, focus, resilience, and interpersonal effectiveness.

Mayur has facilitated Art of Living programs for corporations, individuals, and community groups across Vietnam and India, creating lasting positive change.

Inspiration & Lineage

Gurudev Sri Sri
Ravi Shankar Ji

"Stress is not a requirement for success. You can be dynamic, effective and at peace — all at the same time."

Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar Ji is a global humanitarian, spiritual leader, and peace ambassador who has reached over 500 million people across 180 countries. He is the Founder of The Art of Living Foundation and the International Association for Human Values (IAHV).

Mayur's work is deeply rooted in the teachings, vision, and grace of Gurudev — bringing the universal message of a stress-free, violence-free society to professionals and organizations across the world.

Every program Mayur facilitates carries the spirit of this transformative lineage — empowering people not just to perform better, but to live better.
